Visualizing decision boundaries

Coursera's Developing Data Products project

Paulo Jean. September, 2014.

A thousand words

  • People say that a picture is worth a thousand words.

  • Each classification algorithm generates different decision making rules.

  • With this Shiny application, you can visualize these rules in the form of beautiful decision boundaries plots.

Dataset and models

  • Plots are based on iris dataset, one of the best known datasets in pattern recognition literature.

  • A collection of 7 popular classification algorithms.
